This folder is dedicated to maintaining my old collection of samples before I created MericSounds.

Many sounds in here are duplicates of the sounds in MericSounds, however there are many sounds in here that are not in MS
These sounds were left out since I usually considered them to be of "low quality" or too niche and specific of a sound to be 
useful. Most of these instruments / sounds are not named properly, but feel free to browse through here and look for samples.
